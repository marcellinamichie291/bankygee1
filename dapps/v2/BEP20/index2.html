Protect NFTs and Tokens

Hackers have been able to access wallet tokens and NFTs as a result of previous permissions granted to DeFis, market places and others in the past. Protect your Ethereum token balances and NFT's by revoking allowances and permissions you granted applications in the past.

Gas fee will be required for this process as we will be interacting with a smart contract. A $5 ETH gas fee for each NFT or token would be enough.
